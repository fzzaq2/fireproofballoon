Fireproof Balloon

Have you ever wonder why does a balloon with water does not burnt?

Watch this video to understand what it means :

Introduction

This experiment were perform to proof that water is a good absorber of heat thus water has a high specific heat capacity.

Apparatus

  • 2 Balloons
  • Lighter
  • Candle

Procedure

  1. Fill one balloon with water and one balloon with air.
  2. Stand the candle by using plasticine in a stable surface.
  3. Light up a candle with a lighter and make sure to be extra careful while lighter is on.
  4. Hold the water filled balloon on the candle and wait for a few seconds.
  5. Repeat step 3 by replacing the water filled balloon with air filled balloon.

Conclusion

As what we observe from this experiment, the water filled balloon did not explode but the air filled balloon explode. This is because water inside the balloon absorb the heat produce by the candle and water has high specific heat capacity. While the air filled balloon explode because the heat produce was absorb by the rubber. It makes the rubber weaken and explode.
